Project : UPI Fraud Detection System

In today's digital era, UPI (Unified Payments Interface) has revolutionized the way India transacts. However, it has also become a target for fraudsters. Our team developed a UPI Fraud Detection System to identify and prevent fraudulent transactions using data-driven techniques.

Nitish led the overall project design, handled machine learning model development,
and ensured proper data preprocessing.
Richa focused on the backend architecture,
integrating the fraud detection model with a secure transaction monitoring system.
Vishal managed the real-time alert system,
which immediately notifies users of suspicious activity. He also worked on dashboard analytics.
Priti was responsible for data collection,
visualization, and creating detailed reports of fraudulent patterns and transaction anomalies.

---

Together, we used algorithms like Random Forest and Logistic Regression for accuracy and efficiency. We trained our model on a dataset of both genuine and fraudulent UPI transactions. The system flags high-risk transactions based on behavioral patterns, frequency, and transaction amount. We also added a user feedback loop to refine the model over time. Security, speed, and reliability were our top priorities.

---

Our solution aims to support banks, fintech platforms, and end-users by minimizing loss and improving trust.
This collaborative effort combined our technical strengths and practical thinking.

---

We believe this project holds real-world impact in securing India’s digital economy.

---

We are proud of this innovation and look forward to future improvements and scalability.
